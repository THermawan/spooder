Read Me!

This page will contain a brief explanation of the website and its function, and is geared to direct would-be users to pages appropriate for their use. What it might say could be summed up along the lines of the following.

Welcome to MiniMeds! We are here to help both children and parents better understand stroke (and how to lead healthy lives) in fun and meaningful ways! The following pages contain information about stroke, integrative medicine, and any other big words you might want to know the meanings of. If you think you know your stuff and want to test it out in a cool quiz, go for it! It's right here. If you're a parent and you have some questions, then this is the page for you - but be sure to check out our Frequently Asked Questions page first. If you still have questions after that, you can also contact us. We don't bite, promise.

Oh, and if you're only here to play videogames, that's okay too. We don't judge.

Also, click here to find out more about the awesome people who made this website!
