Snacks and Lunch Box Ideas

To prevent stroke and other illnesses from happening to you and your loved ones, eating healthy can provide all the nutrients your body needs to stay healthy. So, with the help of an adult, grab an apron and start cooking today with these recipes. We have plenty, from muffins to noodles. Parents, don't be afraid to let your kids into the kitchen to have a go.

  • - Iced Fruit Kebabs (Makes 8)
  • - Cheesy Vegetable Muffins (Makes 12)
  • - Hokkien Noodle Salad (Serves 4)
  • - Hummus with Crisp Vegetables (Serves 6)
  • - Apple Pikelets (Makes 24)
  • - Cream Cheese and Carrot Ham Wrap
  • - Beetroot, Carrot and Hummus Sandwich
  • - Apricot Muesli Balls (Makes 22)
  • - Creamy Pasta Salad (Serves 4)
