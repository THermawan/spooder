Our Goal

Our goal is to educate 8-13 year olds on the subject of Integrative Medicine and stroke and how this may be(come) relevant to them at any point in their lives. As such, it is very much our intention to encourage preventative and healthy behaviours from an early age.

We plan to evaluate our success by establishing, very early on, a set of evaluation criteria that we will constantly measure our product against throughout the development of our website by way of progressive evaluation. External feedback is also periodically sought-after and contributes to much of this process, and a final, thorough evaluation will be conducted once the product is complete.

Evaluation Criteria

  • The website must be fully functional and allow all provided content to be accessible by the user.

  • The website must function as a platform for the target audience of 8-13 year olds to learn about Stroke and IM.

  • The website must be visually consistent, easy to navigate and aesthetically pleasing to the target audience, with tools such as brighter colours and graphics to break up any large amounts of information.

  • The website must foster an environment that allows the target audience to find learning about Stroke and IM fun and/or rewarding.
